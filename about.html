About S.M.R Engineering Industries

S.M.R Engineering Industries was established in 2006 and is an ISO 9001:2015 certified precision machining company specializing in CNC turning job work. We supply high-quality turned components to various industries by strictly following standardized quality procedures.

Our quality management system ensures controlled processes, continual improvement, and complete customer satisfaction. Every component we manufacture undergoes rigorous inspection to meet customer specifications and industry standards.

With years of experience in precision machining, we have built a reputation for delivering defect-free components with repeat accuracy. Our commitment to quality and on-time delivery has made us a trusted partner for businesses across multiple sectors.
