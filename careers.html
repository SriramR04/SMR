Career Opportunities

We provide opportunities for skilled professionals and students in mechanical engineering

👷 CNC Turning Operators

We are looking for skilled CNC turning machine operators with experience in precision machining. Join our team to work with advanced CNC equipment and quality-driven processes.

  • Requirements: ITI/Diploma/BE in Mechanical
  • Experience: 0-3 years in CNC turning
  • Skills: Drawing reading, tool setting
  • Benefits: Competitive salary, training

🎓 Training Program

Opportunities for students to gain practical experience in CNC machining, precision manufacturing, and quality control processes through structured training programs.

  • Eligibility: SSLC, HSC, ITI, Diploma or Higher
  • Duration: 3-6 months
  • Training: Hands-on CNC operation & programming
  • Certificate: Experience certificate provided

📚 Internship & Apprenticeship

Structured internship and apprenticeship programs designed to provide practical knowledge and industry exposure to students pursuing mechanical engineering.

  • Program Type: Internship/Apprenticeship
  • Flexible Duration: Based on academic requirements
  • Practical Training: Production floor experience
  • Guidance: Mentorship from experienced staff
