Quality Assurance Practices

Comprehensive quality control measures at every stage of production

🎯 Inspection Procedures

  • First Article Inspection: Complete dimensional verification of the first component from each new setup
  • In-Process Checks: Regular monitoring during production to ensure dimensional consistency
  • Final Inspection: 100% inspection before dispatch to verify all critical dimensions
  • Statistical Process Control: Data-driven monitoring for process stability

📏 Measurement & Calibration

  • Calibrated Instruments: All measuring equipment regularly calibrated to traceable standards
  • Preventive Maintenance: Periodic inspection and servicing of measuring instruments to ensure accuracy
  • In-House Verification: Routine internal checks performed to maintain consistent measurement reliability
  • Quality Inspection Control: Strict monitoring of measurement processes to ensure product conformity

📋 Documentation & Records

  • Material Traceability: Complete tracking from raw material to finished component
  • Inspection Reports: Detailed dimensional reports for customer review
  • Process Parameters: Recording of critical machining parameters
  • Non-Conformance Reports: Documentation of deviations and corrective actions

📦 Cost Optimization & Inventory Control

  • FIFO System: First-In-First-Out method followed to ensure proper material usage and traceability
  • Scrap Control: Continuous monitoring to reduce waste and improve cost efficiency
  • Tool Life Monitoring: Regular tracking to maintain machining accuracy and process stability
  • Inventory Control: Planned stock management to ensure smooth and uninterrupted production
